Design Process

  • Research

    Research the problem, identify the underlying need and create specifications for design.

  • Design

    Design concepts that satisfy the functions and contraints of the underlying need.

  • Prototype

    Prototype a functional and/or aesthetic model based on the design and analyze its performance.

  • Iterate

    Iterate on the design after identifying areas of improvement to better address the need.
